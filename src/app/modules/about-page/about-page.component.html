About

Quick Quote is a non-commercial project that aims to motivate, inspire and benefit people.

We give you easy access to an almost endless list of significant quotes from more than 30,000 authors, scientists, celebrities, musicians, politicians, athletes and famous people from history.

We will be happy if you discover quotes that will inspire you to new achievements, help you in difficult times or decorate any of your conversations.
